Election Timeline Phases

Key milestones in a typical election cycle.

1

Phase 1: Voter Registration

Citizens ensure they are on the electoral roll. Deadlines typically fall 15-30 days before election day.

2

Phase 2: Nomination

Individuals formally declare their candidacy and file the necessary paperwork to appear on the ballot.

3

Phase 3: Campaigning

Candidates present their platforms, participate in debates, and engage with voters.

4

Phase 4: Election Day

Polls open. Voters cast their ballots.

5

Phase 5: Counting

Counting begins immediately after polls close and results are certified.
